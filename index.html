About us

Matha Industries has been established as a manufacturing company in 1987 in Chennai, Tamil Nadu.

We are Manufacturer and supplier of electrical parts, mining parts, fuses, press tools components, industrial pressed components, plastic components, special tools etc...Exclusively for escalators lifts and elevators companies all over the nation.

We have our office and factory at Perambur with limited infrastructure, then slowly grown to this stage with the help of strong Team, Hard Work and Enthusiasm. we are a ISO 9001 to 2015 Certified Company Now we have our main factory at Kuthambakkam, Padur, Chennai 602 -105
